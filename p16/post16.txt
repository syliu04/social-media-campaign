This isn't an activist claim. It's the federal government's own lab.

When the National Institute of Standards and Technology tested face recognition algorithms across demographics, it found false-positive rates 10 to 100 times higher for Asian and African American faces than for white faces, depending on the algorithm.

A false positive in face recognition isn't a glitch. In policing, it can mean the wrong person gets stopped, questioned, or arrested.

When even the government's own testing says the tech is unequal, "trust the algorithm" stops being a neutral statement.
